Social Playbook
Platform-specific content strategy to reach travelers where they already scroll.
Instagram
Content Type
Reels, Carousels, Stories
Tone
Aspirational, visual-first, FOMO-inducing
Posting Cadence
4-5x per week, daily Stories
Audience
25-34, travel-curious, lifestyle-driven
Example Post
"POV: your group chat actually agreed on a destination. Bali, here we come." Carousel of trip highlights with a "Plan yours" CTA in Stories.
TikTok
Content Type
Short-form video, Trends, Duets
Tone
Funny, relatable, trend-aware
Posting Cadence
5-7x per week
Audience
18-28, Gen Z, group travel planners
Example Post
"Things that are harder than planning a group trip: nothing. That's why we built tryps." Trending audio + chaotic group chat screenshots.
Twitter / X
Content Type
Threads, Quotes, Hot takes
Tone
Witty, conversational, meme-aware
Posting Cadence
Daily, real-time engagement
Audience
Tech-forward, early adopters, travel Twitter
Example Post
"group trips have a 0% survival rate in the Notes app. we fixed that."
iMessage
Content Type
Rich link previews, Trip cards, Nudges
Tone
Personal, direct, action-oriented
Frequency
Event-driven, trip lifecycle moments
Audience
Active trip planners and their friends
Example Message
"Your Tulum trip is 80% booked. Just waiting on Jake and Emma to lock in their flights."
