App Design Principles
Four guiding principles that shape every design decision in the tryps app.
01
Martin is the hero
The AI assistant is the primary interface. Every screen should feel like Martin is helping. Users talk to Martin, not menus. The conversational UI is the product.
02
Trip card is the viral hook
The most polished, shareable element. It should make people screenshot and share. Every trip card is a billboard for tryps, designed to spark FOMO and curiosity.
03
Zero-friction engagement
Every action should be one tap. No onboarding walls. No sign-up gates before value. Users should feel the magic before they commit to anything.
04
Value before commitment
Show the trip, the itinerary, the savings before asking for signup. Let people see what they're missing. The product sells itself when you lead with value.
