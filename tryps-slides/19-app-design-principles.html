19 App Design
Design Principles
Four rules that guide every pixel, interaction, and product decision inside Tryps.
01
Martin from Tryps is the hero
The iMessage travel agent is the product. Every screen exists to support the conversation, not replace it. Martin does the heavy lifting so your group chat stays fun.
💬
02
Trip card is the viral hook
The most shareable element in the entire product. It has to look good enough to screenshot, forward, and post. If people don't want to share it, it's not done.
🗺️
03
Zero-friction engagement
Every interaction should feel instant. One tap to vote, one swipe to browse, one link to join. Remove every unnecessary step between curiosity and action.
⚡
04
Value before commitment
See the trip before you sign up. Browse destinations, check prices, read the itinerary. The product earns trust by showing you everything upfront.
✨
