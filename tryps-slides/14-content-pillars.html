Content Pillars
Four strategic themes that guide every piece of content we create.
🧭
Trip Inspiration
Spark wanderlust. Show people places they didn't know they needed to visit. Make the scroll stop and the group chat start buzzing.
Example Topics
- Hidden gems in Southeast Asia
- Weekend trips under $500
- "You need to go here" Reels
- Seasonal destination roundups
- Dream trip mood boards
💡
Planning Tips
Remove friction from group travel planning. Practical, actionable advice that makes the hard parts easy and the easy parts obvious.
Example Topics
- How to split costs without drama
- The 48-hour trip planning sprint
- Booking hacks for group rates
- When to book flights (data-backed)
- Packing lists by destination
❤️
Friend Stories
Real stories from real friend groups. The chaos, the memories, the moments that only happen when you actually go. User-generated, authentic, emotional.
Example Topics
- "How we planned our trip in 20 min"
- Group trip photo dumps
- Before/after trip transformations
- Friendship milestone trips
- Trip disaster stories (funny ones)
📍
Destination Guides
Deep-dive guides that go beyond the tourist traps. Local favorites, hidden spots, the places your friend who lives there would actually take you.
Example Topics
- 72 hours in Tokyo with friends
- Bali on a budget (for real)
- The local's guide to Tulum
- Best group-friendly Airbnbs
- Restaurants with big tables
