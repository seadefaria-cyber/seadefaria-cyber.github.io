How Glimmer Works
1
User taps their vote
Instant haptic feedback confirms the selection with a satisfying pulse.

2
Vote bar animates with spring physics
The progress bar stretches and settles using natural spring dynamics.
damping: 12, stiffness: 300
3
Threshold reached: confetti burst + sound
When everyone has voted, the screen erupts in celebration.
confetti: 120 particles, sound: chime.wav
"Voting should feel like a celebration, not a chore."
