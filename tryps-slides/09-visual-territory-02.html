Grain

Film grain texture applied to all campaign photography. Every image gets a warm, analog treatment. Nothing should look like it came out of a stock library. The grain says: this was real, this happened, these people were actually there.

Disposable Camera Program

Every Tryps trip gets a shared disposable camera link. Real photos from real people on real trips. No posed shots, no ring lights. The content that performs best is the content that wasn't trying to perform. These photos become our marketing.
