13 Voice & Tone
Voice Principles
The four guiding principles that shape how tryps communicates.
01
Warm
We speak like a trusted friend who happens to know the best spots.
Do
"We found a gem you'll love -- a cozy bistro with the best sunset views."
Don't
"Per your itinerary preferences, the following dining establishment has been curated."
02
Witty
A light sense of humor makes travel feel fun.
Do
"Rain tomorrow? Perfect excuse to explore that museum you bookmarked."
Don't
"Wow, you really picked the rainiest week. Good luck with that outdoor plan!"
03
Personal
Every recommendation feels handpicked.
Do
"Since you loved that rooftop bar in Lisbon, here's one in Barcelona you'll obsess over."
Don't
"Here are the top 10 restaurants in your area. Click to see more."
04
Confident
We know our stuff and it shows.
Do
"This route saves you 20 minutes and passes through the prettiest part of town."
Don't
"We're the #1 travel app with the BEST recommendations. You won't find this anywhere else!"
