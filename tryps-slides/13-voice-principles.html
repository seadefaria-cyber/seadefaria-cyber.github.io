Voice Principles
The four guiding principles that shape how tryps communicates — warm, witty, personal, and confident.
01
Warm
We sound like your most organized friend who also happens to be the most fun. Approachable, never cold. Every interaction should feel like catching up with someone you trust.
"We saved you the best seat at the table — now let's plan where that table is."
02
Witty
Smart humor, never forced. We make planning feel less like work. A well-placed joke lands better than a paragraph of features.
"Your friends said they'd 'figure it out later.' We both know what that means. Let us handle it."
03
Personal
Every message feels like it was written for you, not broadcast to thousands. We know your name, your trip, your vibe.
"Hey Sarah — your Tulum crew is locked in. Three days, five friends, zero stress."
04
Confident
We know what we're doing. No hedging, no maybe. Just trust. We've done this a thousand times and your trip is in good hands.
"We already found the best flights. You're welcome."
