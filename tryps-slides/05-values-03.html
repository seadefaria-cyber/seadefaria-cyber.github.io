03 Brand Values
03
Come Correct
If Apple wouldn't ship it, we don't ship it. Every pixel, every interaction, every loading state. The bar is not "good enough." The bar is "would this make someone stop and say damn, this is nice?" We hold ourselves to the standard of the best products on the planet, not the best travel apps.
04
Clean
If it's not worth screenshotting, it's not done. Every screen, every card, every notification should look intentional. No clutter, no visual noise, no "we'll fix it later." Clean is not minimal for the sake of it. Clean means every element earned its place. Nothing extra, nothing missing.
