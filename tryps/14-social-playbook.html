14 SOCIAL MEDIA

Social Playbook

Channels
Twitter / X
Real-time commentary and personality. Where Tryps has a voice, not just a feed. Witty, conversational, the friend who always has the best travel take.
Instagram
The grid is our storefront. Every post earns its spot. Stories are behind the scenes, raw, unfiltered. The grid stays curated.
TikTok
Authentic, never polished. If it looks like it had a production meeting, it doesn't belong here. Real trips, real chaos, real people.
Pinterest
Trip inspo boards that actually convert. Destination mood boards, packing guides, group trip aesthetics. Long-tail discovery engine.
Rules
01
Grid stays pristine
Every Instagram grid post is reviewed for visual cohesion. No one-offs, no random reposts. The grid is the brand's first impression.
02
Trip-first content
Every piece of content should make someone want to plan a trip. If it doesn't spark that feeling, it doesn't post.
03
50/50 lifestyle and product
Half the feed is aspirational travel content. Half shows the product in action. Never let one dominate the other.
04
No emojis in system copy
Emojis are for user-generated content and casual replies. Official Tryps copy, captions, and marketing materials stay clean.
