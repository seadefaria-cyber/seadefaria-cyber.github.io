14 SOCIAL MEDIA

Content Pillars

📸
User Stories
Real trips, real people. The best marketing is a group of friends having the time of their lives and telling everyone about it. No scripts, no setups.
Examples
"How we planned Bali in 20 minutes"
Group trip photo dumps
Before and after trip energy
✨
Normal People, Amazing Things
Aspirational but attainable. Not influencers in first class. Regular friend groups doing incredible things together. The viewer should think: "that could be us."
Examples
College friends reunite in Portugal
Birthday trip on a real budget
The friend who finally said yes
🌍
Trip Inspiration
Destinations that spark action, not just daydreaming. Every post should make someone drop a pin in the group chat. Show the place, set the vibe, make it feel possible.
Examples
Hidden gems under $1,500
Seasonal destination guides
"You need to go here" Reels
📍
Destination Guides
Practical, opinionated, and written like your friend who actually lives there. No generic top-10 lists. The restaurants with big tables, the neighborhoods worth staying in, the stuff Google won't tell you.
Examples
72 hours in Tokyo with friends
The local's guide to Tulum
Best group-friendly Airbnbs
