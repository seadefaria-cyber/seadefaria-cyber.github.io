03 Brand Values
01
Obvious
Every screen should explain itself. Zero friction, zero tutorials, zero "getting started" guides. If someone opens Tryps for the first time and has to think about what to tap, we failed. The best UX is the one nobody notices. It just works, and you're already planning.
02
Shareworthy
Trip cards are the product and the marketing. When someone drops a Tryps link in the group chat, it should feel like a gift, not a chore. The link preview alone should make people excited. Every share is a viral hook. Every trip card is a billboard for the brand.
